Who We Are

At KMC Projects, we specialise in delivering tailored hydraulic solutions across design, consultancy, project management, and training. With over 20 years of hands-on industry experience, Chartered Engineer Kevin McCaffrey leads a team that understands the critical role hydraulics play in system efficiency and operational reliability. From concept development to commissioning, we work collaboratively to ensure every design is robust, compliant, and optimised for real-world performance.


Whether you’re launching a new infrastructure project, upgrading legacy systems, or developing in-house capability, KMC Projects offers a streamlined, expert-led approach. We pride ourselves on translating complex hydraulic challenges into practical, effective outcomes — helping clients reduce risk, increase efficiency, and stay ahead of industry demands.


About Kevin McCaffrey

Kevin began his previous company, Excel Hydraulics, in 1998. What began with Kevin tinkering in his garage led to a thriving business, acquired in 2016. His passion for fixing problems continues in his new enterprise. Kevin is renowned for his innovation and getting the best out of people. He is a specialist in fluid power engineering, hydraulics, and problem-solving.
